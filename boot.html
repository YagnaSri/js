Benefits of learning German.

German is the second most-used language in science. Germany is the third biggest contributor to investigations and offers places to study postdoctoral studies for international scientists.Furthermore, Germany is the third biggest economy in the world, and it is home to leading corporations of the global market such as Audi, Siemens, Volkswagen, Adidas y Lufthansa. Some even call Berlin the “Silicon Valley” of Europe. Mastering German thus opens the door to a plethora of business opportunities in universities or in companies with branches either in your country or abroad
